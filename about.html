Building the Future of Energy

RAYS Global Energy Pvt. Ltd. is a premier manufacturer of high-performance EV Batteries and Smart EV Chargers, dedicated to accelerating the global transition to electric mobility.

With over two decades of engineering excellence, we combine cutting-edge technology, superior safety standards, and sustainable manufacturing practices to deliver world-class energy solutions that power modern transportation.
